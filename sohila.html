The club was founded on 24 April 1907

Al-Ahly Club is located in the Arab Republic of Egypt and its main headquarters are at 3 Saleh Selim Street, El-Gezira, Cairo Governorate

Al-Ahly Club currently has 3 branches:

  • The first in Nasr City, Hassan Al-Mamoun Street, 6th District, Cairo Governorate
  • The second in the Sixth of October City, Sheikh Zayed City, Giza Governorate
  • The third is under construction in New Cairo
